Life at VIT-AP

Our 150-acre campus in Amaravathi offers a perfect blend of academic rigor and vibrant campus life. From state-of-the-art labs to sports complexes, from cultural festivals to tech fests — there's always something happening at VIT-AP.

With students from 28+ states and 45+ countries, you'll be part of a truly diverse and inclusive community that celebrates differences and builds lasting friendships.

Join Us →
Facilities

World-Class Facilities

Everything you need for a comfortable and productive campus experience.

🏠

Hostels

Separate hostels for men and women with AC/non-AC rooms, Wi-Fi, laundry, and 24/7 security. Capacity for 10,000+ students.

🍽️

Dining

Multiple mess halls serving vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisines. Food courts with North Indian, South Indian, and continental options.

🏟️

Sports Complex

Cricket ground, football field, basketball courts, tennis courts, swimming pool, indoor badminton, table tennis, and gymnasium.

📚

Central Library

Multi-story library with 100,000+ books, digital resources, e-journals, quiet study zones, and group discussion rooms.

🏥

Healthcare Center

On-campus medical center with qualified doctors, pharmacy, ambulance service, and tie-ups with major hospitals.

🚌

Transport

University bus service connecting campus to Vijayawada, Guntur, and surrounding areas. Well-maintained roads and parking.

Student Life

Clubs & Activities

Over 50+ student clubs and chapters to explore your passions beyond academics.

💻

Tech Clubs

Coding Club, Robotics Club, AI/ML Club, Cybersecurity Club, Open Source Society, and IEEE/ACM Student Chapters.

🎭

Cultural Clubs

Music Club, Dance Club, Drama Club, Photography Club, Art Club, and Literary Society.

🤝

Social Clubs

NSS, Rotaract, Environmental Club, Social Awareness Club, and community outreach programs.

🏆

Sports Clubs

Cricket, Football, Basketball, Badminton, Athletics, Chess, and E-Sports teams competing at national level.

🚀

Entrepreneurship Cell

Startup incubation, business plan competitions, mentor connect programs, and investor pitch sessions.

🎪

Annual Festivals

Gravitas (Technical Fest), Riviera (Cultural Fest), and multiple department-level symposiums and hackathons.
