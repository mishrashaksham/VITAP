How To Apply

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to join VIT-AP University.

1

Register for VITEEE

Create an account on the VIT admissions portal and register for the VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE).

2

Appear for Examination

Take the VITEEE exam at your nearest test center. The exam tests Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, English, and Aptitude.

3

Counseling & Seat Allotment

Based on your rank, participate in the online counseling process and choose your preferred program and campus.

4

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the admission fee, submit required documents, and confirm your seat at VIT-AP University.

5

Welcome to VIT-AP!

Attend the orientation program and begin your academic journey at our beautiful Amaravathi campus.

Fee Structure & Scholarships

Quality education made accessible through competitive fees and generous scholarships.

📋

B.Tech Programs

Annual tuition fee: ₹2,00,000 – ₹2,50,000 depending on specialization. Hostel and mess charges extra.

📊

MBA Program

Annual tuition fee: ₹3,00,000. Includes access to industry workshops, guest lectures, and placement support.

🎓

Scholarships

Merit scholarships covering 20% to 100% of tuition fees based on VITEEE rank, board exam scores, and special categories.
